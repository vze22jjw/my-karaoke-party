The guest interface is divided into 4 main tabs to manage the party experience.

## 1. Playing (Monitor)
* **Current Song:** Shows the active music video, title, and artist.
* **Up Next:** Displays the next 3 songs in the Fair Queue.
* **Status:** Indicates if the player is "Playing", "Paused", or "Idle".

## 2. Add Song (Music/Plus)
* **Search:** Search YouTube directly for karaoke tracks.
* **Queue:** Add songs to your personal queue.
* **Fairness:** You can add multiple songs, but the "Fair Queue" algorithm ensures you rotate with other singers.

## 3. Suggestions (Lightbulb)
* **Theme:** See the host's suggested theme (e.g., "80s Rock").
* **Top Played:** A list of the most popular songs in this party's history.
* **Top Singers:** A leaderboard showing the most active singers and their **Applause Score**.

## 4. Singers (Users)
* **List:** See all active participants in the room.
* **Queue Visibility:** Expand a singer's card to see what they have queued up.
* **Applause (New!):** * Tap the **ğŸ‘ button** in the header to open the **Applause Controller**.
    * Spam the big applause button to send hearts and cheers to the current singer!
    * This uses a robust REST API to ensure your cheers always get through, even on slow connections.

---

## Join Flow
* **Manual Code:** Enter the 4-letter code (e.g., `ABCD`) if you are near the host screen.
* **Active Parties:** Click "Find a Party" to open a drawer listing all public, active parties on the network.
